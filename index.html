Draft Board

Ranked by total score (Dash + Agility + Catch, max ~12). Players ranked without full scores show NS. A red Avoid tag marks players to steer clear of. Anyone tagged Hat Pick — automatically for not-yet-evaluated players, or manually by setting a card's Coach look to Hat Pick — always sorts to the very bottom, below every ranked player. Tap Edit on any card to update the player's name, scores, tags, or notes — everything saves automatically. Drag a card (press and hold on a phone) to reorder. On draft day, hit Mark Drafted and pick which team got him — your own picks land under My Team below the board, everyone else's land under Drafted elsewhere. Use Teams to rename your team or add other coaches, and Look Tags to rename the 6/23, 7/9, Hat Pick, or No Look categories.
